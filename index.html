Tab Recorder

Record your meetings, kept on your computer.

A dead simple recorder for Google Meet. Click record, and the call is saved as a video file on your own machine. No account, no cloud, no AI notetaker.

Local first No account No AI Follows your Meet mute Optional Google Drive backup

What it does

  • ✓ Records the meeting tab to a video file on your computer.
  • ✓ Shows a recorder button right inside the Google Meet call.
  • ✓ Mutes your mic in the recording when you mute yourself in Meet.
  • ✓ Keeps a tidy list of your recordings to play, rename, or delete.
  • ✓ Optional: back a copy up to your own Google Drive, only if you turn it on.

Your privacy

  • ✓ Recordings never leave your computer unless you choose Google Drive backup.
  • ✓ No analytics, no tracking, nothing sent to us. Ever.
  • ✓ Google Drive backup uses the least access possible: the app can only see files it made.
